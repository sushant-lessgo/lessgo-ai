About Vestria

Dressing excellence, empowering the people who wear it.

We are more than a uniform supplier. From a single Bur Dubai workshop in 2009, Vestria has grown into a full-service manufacturer outfitting professionals across the region.

Every programme is built on the same foundations — considered materials, precise construction and an unbending commitment to your complete satisfaction. Our value-added services are what separate us from the trade: we don't just sell garments, we run the wardrobe your operation depends on.
