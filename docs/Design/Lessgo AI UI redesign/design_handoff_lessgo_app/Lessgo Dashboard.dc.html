5 morning workouts you can do in 20 minutes
No gym, no excuses. These five quick routines fit into even the busiest morning — and they’re enough to build real momentum before your day begins.
image
1. The 4-minute wake-up flow
Start with bodyweight squats, arm circles, and a light jog in place. The goal isn’t intensity — it’s circulation. Four minutes here primes your body for everything that follows and shakes off the morning stiffness faster than a cup of coffee.
2. Core in five
A short plank circuit — front plank, side planks, and mountain climbers — hits your whole midsection without a single piece of equipment. Two rounds is plenty to feel switched on.
